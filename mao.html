MEET MAOMAO

Introduction

Hello! My name is Maomao, and I am 4 years old! I was rescued as a newborn kitten with my siblings from Hawaii and brought to Seattle, Washington. I was cared for by the Seattle Humane Society before being adopted in December 2019 by a really nice family. Now, my family consists of me, my mom, dad, and two sisters.
My hobbies are sleeping, running outside, sunbathing, and visiting other cats in the neighborhood to play. I also like meeting new people and chasing after anything that moves. I absolutely HATE showers and new environments, and I don't like it when my family doesn't give me enough attention.
